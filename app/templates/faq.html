Semi-formal beach wedding attire.

For men, jackets, waist coats and ties are acceptable but completely optional due to the high temperatures. Trousers and a button-down shirt with an open collar will be suitable. Shoes can be close-toe loafers or something equally semi-formal.

For women, tea and maxi length dresses with dressy sandals. The wedding ceremony is taking place on the beach, so keep this in mind when choosing your footwear and length of your dress.

We expect the temperature to be 25-28 degrees, so please dress with this in mind.

We realise that comming all this way for our wedding day is a lot to ask, so gifts are not expected. Your presence is present enough!

Send an email to lewis@nplgwedding.com with your changes and we will handle it!

There are several other hotels and resorts between the Atlantica Beach Resort and the nearby town of Kardamaina. The closest is the Atlantica Belvedere Resort or the Porto Bello Royal. Alternatively, Kardamaina will have plenty of smaller hotels and bed and breakfasts available.
